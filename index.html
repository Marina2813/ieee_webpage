About Us


IEEE SB MITS Chapter is a dynamic community dedicated to fostering innovation, promoting academic excellence, and empowering aspiring engineers. As an integral part of the prestigious Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, we are committed to advancing technology and shaping the future of our rapidly evolving world.


Our chapter serves as a platform for students to engage in meaningful discussions, hands-on experiences, and collaborative projects that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. With a diverse range of activities and initiatives, we strive to create an environment where students can explore their passion for engineering, develop their skills, and forge lifelong connections.In our ever-evolving digital landscape, staying at the forefront of technological advancements is essential. Through our wide range of events, including hackathons, competitions, and industry talks, we ensure that you are equipped with the latest skills and insights needed to thrive in the fast-paced world of innovation. Join us as we bridge the gap between theory and practice, and unlock the vast potential within you.


Join us at IEEE SB MITS Chapter and become part of a vibrant community that fosters innovation, empowers engineers, and shapes the future. Together, let's embrace the possibilities, push boundaries, and create a world driven by technological advancements and excellence.
